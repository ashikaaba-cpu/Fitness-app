Nutrition

Fuel Your Performance

Nutrition is the foundation of all results. These evidence-based tips will optimize your energy, recovery, and body composition.

💧
Stay Hydrated
Drink 2–3 liters of water daily. Add electrolytes during intense sessions. Hydration directly impacts strength output and recovery speed.
🥩
Prioritize Protein
Aim for 1.6–2.2g of protein per kg of bodyweight. Lean meats, eggs, legumes, and dairy are your muscle-building allies.
🍚
Time Your Carbs
Eat complex carbs 1–2 hours before training for sustained energy. Oats, rice, and sweet potatoes are excellent choices.
🥑
Healthy Fats
Don't fear fats. Avocados, nuts, and olive oil support hormone production and joint health — essential for long-term training.
🌙
Post-Workout Meal
Consume a protein + carb meal within 45 minutes of training. This anabolic window maximizes muscle repair and glycogen restoration.
🚫
Minimize Processed Foods
Cut out refined sugars and trans fats. Whole, minimally processed foods reduce inflammation and improve body composition over time.
